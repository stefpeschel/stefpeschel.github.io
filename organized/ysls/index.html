Young Statisticians Lecture Series - Modern Statistical Methods for Life Sciences

Apr 18, 2023
Project Website

The Young Statisticians Lecture Series is organized by the Early Career Working Group (AG Nachwuchs) of the IBS-DR. This online lecture series should give students of Statistics and Biometry the opportunity to present their Master’s thesis or current PhD project in a larger and more flexible time frame than is usual at conferences.

Past and future talks can be found here.

I mainly organize the event and also do the moderation from time to time.
